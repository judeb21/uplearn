If You Want Your Child To Be Kind, Considerate And Helpful To Others

Try These

Teach them emotions and empathy.

For emotions, let your child copy different emotions from you such as joy, anger, excitement, pain, disappointment, tiredness, terror, mischief, weirdness, etc

Play ‘identify the emotion game’ by making faces or holding up papers with different smileys. This will help them to easily differentiate emotions and express better.

For empathy, come up with different scenarios by asking your child how other people might feel when certain things happen. You can try different scenarios and examples each time.
