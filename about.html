What Uplearn is

Uplearn is a platform aimed at helping teachers and parents find tools and resources that can improve the learning of the children under their care.

With the Uplellllarn cards, parents, teachers and students can see areas where the students have strengths, weaknesses, opportunities to improve and areas they should be mindful of.

Who Uplearn is for

The Uplearn platform is open to teachers, parents and students at no cost.

This platform was created by DODO Design Agency, a Design, Strategy and Innovation Agency based in Lagos Nigeria as a means to partner with the Educational system in Nigeria to help develop students into individuals excelling in every chosen field.
